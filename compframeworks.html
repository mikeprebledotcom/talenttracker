Competency Frameworks

Align employee development with business goals, improve performance, and drive growth

A competency framework is a structured approach to defining and measuring the skills, knowledge, and abilities that are required to perform a specific job or role within an organization.
It provides a clear roadmap for employees to follow as they develop and grow in their careers, and helps HR teams and business leaders align employee development with company goals.

Talent Tracker can help your organization create a customized competency framework that accurately reflects the unique needs and expectations of your business. By partnering with us, you can ensure that your employees are aware of the skills and capabilities necessary to drive success and drive your business forward.
